Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Parvez Khakimov, a 20-year-old student from Bukhara, Uzbekistan. Currently, I am studying Computer Information Systems for Business at MDIS in Tashkent. My love for technology began at a young age, and I am passionate about utilizing it to solve real-world problems and build impactful solutions.

I specialize in web development and have experience working with various front-end technologies such as React.js, JavaScript, and TypeScript. I enjoy building intuitive, user-friendly applications that help improve business processes and provide seamless experiences to users. Every project I take on is an opportunity to learn and refine my skills further.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, playing sports like football, and spending time with friends and family. I believe that a balance between personal interests and professional growth is key to success.

I am fluent in Uzbek, Russian, and English, which has helped me connect with a diverse range of people and cultures. My goal is to continue learning and expanding my expertise while making a meaningful contribution to the world through technology.
